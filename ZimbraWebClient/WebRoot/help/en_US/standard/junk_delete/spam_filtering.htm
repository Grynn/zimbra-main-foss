Using the Junk folder

Most filtering of unsolicited automated email (aka "spam" or "junk mail") is handled by a spam filter before those email messages reach your Inbox. Mail that might possibly be junk mail, but is not certain to be junk, is placed in your Junk folder. You can review these messages and either move them out of the Junk folder or delete them. If you don't move or delete the messages, they are purged after a number of days as specified by your administrator.

  • If you find that you are receiving a large number of unwanted email messages, contact your administrator. Reporting mail as spam helps your email administrator to fine-tune any spam filtering that may be in use at your site.

Move messages out of Junk folder

  1. Click on the Junk folder in the Navigation pane

  2. Check the boxes next to the messages to move

  3. In the Move to...  field, select the folder where the messages should be moved.

  4. Click Move.

To delete messages from the Junk folder

  1. Click on the Junk folder in the Navigation pane.

  2. If you do to delete specific messages, check the boxes next to the messages to be deleted.

  3. Click Delete.

To delete all messages from the Junk folder

  1. Click on the Junk folder in the Navigation pane.

  2. Click Empty Junk on the toolbar..
