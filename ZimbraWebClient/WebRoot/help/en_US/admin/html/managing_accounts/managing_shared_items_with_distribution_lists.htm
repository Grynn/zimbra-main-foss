Managing Shared Items with Distribution Lists

Distribution lists can be used to share  mail folders, address books, calendars, task lists, Documents notebook folders, and Briefcase folders with all members of the list. Everyone has the same share privileges that the grantee defined for the shared folder.  When members are removed from the distribution list, their share privileges are revoked.

Administrators can manage the shares from the distribution list account's Shared tab.  

Users must notify the administrator that they have shared a folder with the distribution list. To make the shared item available to members of the DL, the administrator publishes the shared folder in the DL's Shares tab. When a new shared folder is published, existing members of the DL are automatically notified of the new share.

Automatic notification to new members to the DL can be enabled so that new members receive an email with a list of the shared folders.
