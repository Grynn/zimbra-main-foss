Setting the default account on Yahoo! Zimbra Desktop

When you open Yahoo! Zimbra Desktop, your accounts are displayed on the left in the order that you set them up. The first account is the default account. This is the account that is opened when you launch Yahoo! Zimbra Desktop. You can change which account is the default.

  1. Click Account Setup on the Yahoo! Zimbra Desktop. The My Accounts page displays a list of your accounts. The first account in the list is the default.

  2. To change the default account, click Default under the account name you want as the default. That  account is moved to the top.  

 

Besides being the account that is opened when Yahoo! Zimbra Desktop is displayed, the default account is the account used if Yahoo! Zimbra Desktop is configured as the default mail client.
