Setting IM preferences

Use the options on the Preferences>Instant Messaging tab as follows:  Be sure to click Save to save any changes you make to your preferences.

  • Login. Check the box if you want to log  into IM automatically when you sign into the @ClientNameLong@ . If the box is not checked, you manually sign in by changing your status from offline to online from the status menu.

  • Notifications. Choose options for seeing IM notifications

    • Enable instant notifications shows brief pop up messages to notify other's of your contacts’ status.

    • Display presence notifications displays a brief pop up to notify you if a buddy is online or offline.

    • Display notification when a buddy changes status displays a brief pop up to notify you if a buddy changes their status in @CompanyName@ IM.

  • When a message arrives. Choose settings to be displayed on IM.

    • Play a sound plays sounds when a message arrives. You need the Quicktime or Windows Media Player plug in installed to hear sounds.

    • Flash the browser title flashes the browser title if you are working in a different browser tab or browser window and receive an IM message.

    • Show a popup notification.  You must have Yahoo! BrowserPlus installed.

  • Chat. Customize your chat experience.

    • Save chat history saves your chat transcripts. You can access saved chats in the Chats folder under the Mail tab

    • Report idle status reports you as idle to your buddies if you are away from @ClientNameShort@ for the amount of time specified in Idle timeout.

  • Idle timeout lets you choose the amount of time to pass before changing your status. Your status changes according to your specifications under the Set presence option.

  • Set presence lets you choose the status to be displayed when you do not access IM. The status changes according to the amount of time specified under Idle timeout.
