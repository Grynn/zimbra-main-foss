Scheduling resources

Resources are considered a location (meeting room, building, or other location) or equipment (audio/visual) that can be scheduled for a meeting. When you set up a meeting, to schedule a resource, you select the resource and "invite" it to the meeting.  The resource receives the invite and if it is free, accepts the meeting.  

Administrators set up resource accounts with the email addresses and configure availability. You receive an email from the resource that either accepts or declines the meeting. A meeting is declined if the resource already has a meeting scheduled for that time.  If a recurring meeting has scheduled a resource, but has cancelled several of the dates, you receive an email showing which dates conflict. In this case, the resource is reserved for your dates unless you click Cancel.
