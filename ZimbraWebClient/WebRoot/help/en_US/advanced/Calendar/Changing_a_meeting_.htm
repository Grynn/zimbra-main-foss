Changing a meeting you organized

You can change and delete meetings you organized. You can send an email to attendees to notify them of the changes or select not to notify attendees of the changes.

  1. Right-click on the meeting to change and select Open. The Appointment Details page is displayed.

  2. Make your changes. You can change the schedule, add and remove attendees, add notes in the Text field and add attachments.

If the changes you made do not require notifying users, uncheck Send Notification Mail. Send Notification Mail gives you the option to make changes to a meeting without notifying all the attendees of the change.

If you are sending an email to attendees of the changes but do not need them to respond to the message, uncheck Request Responses.

  1. Click Save.
