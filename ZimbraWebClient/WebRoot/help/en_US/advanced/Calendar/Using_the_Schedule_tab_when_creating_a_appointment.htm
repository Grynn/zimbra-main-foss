Using the Schedule tab when creating an appointment

The Schedule tab can be used to help schedule a meeting time when most attendees are available. Each person you are inviting is listed with a timeline showing the free and busy periods for that person,  if the attendee's schedule is known. You can adjust the day and times to review different availability.

You can also mark the person's attendance to the meeting as required or optional. Click on the Icon next to the name to select the option.
